The best movies according to Me are

My top three movies of all-time.

Dangal(2016)

Great representation of female child's struggle for becoming a world champion in wrestling driven by her fathers relentless efforts and ambition.

Bajrangi Bhaijaan(2015)

Extraordinary tale of a man and a lost child and the man's unshakable stuborness and motive to make the child reach her home in an enemy country.

Sita Ramam(2022)

A very emotional and heart wrenching story of a soldier and his lover,who were denied love by faith and destiny.
